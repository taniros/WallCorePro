# Google Play ASO Assets – Morning & Night Wishes

Use these assets when publishing to the Google Play Console.

## Required assets

| Asset | File | Size | Usage |
|-------|------|------|-------|
| **Feature graphic** | `play_feature_graphic.png` | 1024 × 500 px | Banner at top of store listing |
| **App icon** | `play_store_icon.png` or `play_store_icon_512.png` | 512 × 512 px | Store listing icon |

## Upload instructions

1. **Feature graphic** (1024×500)  
   Store listing → Main store listing → Feature graphic

2. **App icon** (512×512)  
   Store listing → Main store listing → App icon

3. **Optional:** Screenshots  
   - Phone: 320–3840 px on shortest side  
   - 7-inch tablet: 320–3840 px on shortest side  
   - 10-inch tablet: 320–3840 px on shortest side  

## ASO tips

- **Title:** Use "Morning & Night Wishes" (30 chars max)
- **Short description:** ~80 chars, include keywords: morning wishes, good night, wallpapers, greetings
- **Full description:** Expand on features, benefits, and keywords (4000 chars max)

---
*Assets use accent color #FF6F00 and sun/moon theme.*
